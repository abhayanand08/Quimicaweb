Chem-It-Out

The Quimica Society hosted its technical carnival, CHEM-IT-OUT, at TechMahotsav’23, offering a thrilling lineup of events under the department’s banner. This tech fest served as a launch pad for technology enthusiasts to showcase their creativity and intellect through competitive events, guest lectures, seminars, workshops, and exhibitions. Quimica provided a refreshing space for students to pause, gain new insights, and demonstrate their technical prowess.
